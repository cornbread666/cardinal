Tangleroot Calculator
This tool is designed for players of the medieval point-and-click MMORPG Old School Runescape.

To use the calculator, simply input which crop types you plan to harvest and how many you plan to harvest each day.
For crops with multiple patches that take more than 24 hours to grow, use the amount of patches divided by the number of days.

Example: Hardwood trees take three days to grow, but there are three patches, giving an average of one tree per day.
This tool was inspired by RenderScape's Tangleroot simulator video on YouTube.

As I am not a professional statistician, if you notice any calculations that appear incorrect, or if you have anything (nice) to say, please feel free to contact me at cardinalsudoku@gmail.com.
